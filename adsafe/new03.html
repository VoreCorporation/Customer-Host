The Major Advantages Of Rapid Prototyping

Rapid prototyping can be defined as a modeling technique which improves and speeds up the development of new products. It is a process that uses computer aided tools and techniques such as 3D printing and stereo lithography to create physical product models for production tooling and analysis. The process can be very helpful to product designers, component suppliers and even manufacturers and comes with a number of advantages to all who choose to use the technique.
